Common Questions

Everything you need to know before DevConf 2026.

When and where is DevConf 2026? +

DevConf 2026 runs for three days this fall at the Moscone Center in San Francisco. Exact dates and the full venue address are on the Venue page, along with nearby hotel recommendations.

What's included in my ticket? +

Every ticket tier includes access to keynote sessions and the main expo floor. Pro and Team passes add workshop seating, recorded sessions, and networking events — see the pricing section above for a full breakdown by tier.

Can I get a refund or transfer my ticket? +

Tickets are fully refundable up to 30 days before the event and transferable to a colleague at any time — just email support@devconf2026.com with the new attendee's details.

Will sessions be recorded? +

Yes. Pro and Team ticket holders get on-demand access to every recorded talk, with Pro passes keeping that access for life and Standard passes for 30 days after the event.

Is there a hackathon or hands-on track? +

A 24-hour hackathon runs alongside the main conference, open to all ticket holders. Teams of up to four can enter, and mentors from our speaker lineup will be on the floor throughout.

Do you offer group or student discounts? +

Groups of 5 or more save 15% automatically at checkout, and students with a valid .edu email get 50% off Standard passes. Reach out to sales@devconf2026.com for team invoicing.
